Subject: Launch Announcement: Two-Day National-Level Hackathon on AI and Cybersecurity Innovations
Dear Students,
RV University is pleased to announce a Two-Day National-Level Hackathon on “AI and Cybersecurity Innovations”, to be conducted at the RV University Mysore Road Campus, Bengaluru, Karnataka, commencing on 14 January 2025.
This hackathon is designed to provide a national platform for undergraduate and postgraduate engineering students to ideate, innovate, and build intelligent solutions addressing real-world cybersecurity challenges. The objective is to encourage hands-on application of artificial intelligence and machine learning in strengthening cyber defense mechanisms while promoting ethical and responsible innovation.
The event will be held on campus over two consecutive days. Key focus areas include AI-driven security solutions, ethical hacking, cyber threat and intrusion detection, malware analysis, and machine learning applications in cybersecurity.
The hackathon is open to UG and PG Engineering students from all departments, with team sizes ranging from 2 to 4 members. Interdisciplinary participation is encouraged.
Registration is completely free, and the registration deadline is 13 January 2025. Interested participants may register using the following link: [Registration Link]. Further event details will be shared with registered participants via email. For students traveling from outside Bengaluru, transport, accommodation, and food will be provided.
Attractive prizes, merit awards, and participation certificates will be awarded.
